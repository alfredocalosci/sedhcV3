The International Federation of Construction History was created in 2018 to bring together Societies from around the world devoted specifically to the study of Construction History.  

Representatives from each Society meet each year to exchange news of forthcoming events, organise the International Congresses on Construction History and recognise new societies that have been formed.  

Membership is open to only societies of Construction History that meet the Federation’s membership criteria.
