The Construction History Society is delighted to announce the First Construction History Conference will be held on Friday 11th and Saturday 12th of April in collaboration with the University of Cambridge Department of Architecture at Queens' College, Cambridge. The conference will bring together speakers from across the world to discuss the latest research in the History of Construction.
