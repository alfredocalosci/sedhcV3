The Ècoles nationales supèrieures d'architecture Paris-Malaquais, Paris La Villette and Versailles as well as the Conservatoire national des arts et métiers (CNAM) invite you to participate in the 4th International Congress on Construction History. After Madrid (2003), Cambridge (2006) and Cottbus (2009), this event will take place in Paris, from Tuesday 3 July to Saturday 7 July 2012.
