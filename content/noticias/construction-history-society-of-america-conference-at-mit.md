The Construction History Society of America (CHSA) will host a two-day meeting at MIT on **November 2-3**, 2012.  

The first day will address the broad subject of American construction in the 19th and 20th centuries, and Saturday, November 3rd will focus entirely on research related to **Guastavino** vaulting.
