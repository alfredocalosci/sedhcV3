The Conference will be organized with plenary sessions with invited speakers and parallel sessions of selected papers arranged according to the relevant topics.

A book of abstracts and the electronic version of the proceedings will be distributed to all participants. If a company or organization has an interest in masonry, a variety of opportunities for sponsorships or exhibitions is available. Please contact the organizers for further details.
